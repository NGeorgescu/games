Clobber

A 5×6 board of wazirs, packed in a checkerboard. Every move clobbers an orthogonally-adjacent enemy stone. You must move — the last player able to capture wins.

Board. 5 files (A–E) × 6 ranks (1–6), all 30 squares filled in a checkerboard; A1 is a black wazir.

Move. Slide one of your wazirs one step (up/down/left/right) onto an adjacent enemy wazir, removing it. That's the only kind of move — every move is a capture.

Winning. White moves first. If it's your turn and none of your stones touches an enemy, you have no move and you lose — so the last player to clobber wins.

Tip. Tap one of your stones, then tap a highlighted enemy to clobber it.
