Step 0 — reality check first

Some of what your commander naturally wants you to do is a slow, non-degen strategy. Tribal or proliferate are fine bracket-2 (maybe pushed to bracket-3) plans — but cat tribal or proliferating poison tokens gets run over in a real pod. Treat any "build-around" text as flavor text if it's slow. Find the fastest, most abusable line your colors allow (sac loops, altars, stax + a tight combo) and build toward that.
