Connect 4 Strategy

Connect 4 is a solved game — with perfect play the first player wins, but only from the center. Learn the ideas that get you there: center control, threats, double threats, traps, and the deep odd/even (odd-hole / even-hole) threat theory. Each idea has a short lesson and a board drill.

Solved game: James Dow Allen & Victor Allis, 1988. Theory after Allis's thesis. · ← all games
