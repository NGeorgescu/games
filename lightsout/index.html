Lights Out

The 5×5 puzzle where every tap flips a light and its four neighbours, and you win when the whole board goes dark. It looks like trial and error. It isn't — underneath it's a piece of linear algebra, and a simple procedure (chasing the lights) cracks any solvable board. This course teaches the trick, then the mathematics that makes it work: why press-order is irrelevant, why some boards are impossible, and how to finish in the fewest taps.
