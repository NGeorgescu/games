Tiny House Chess

A pocket chess variant on a 4×4 board with four fairy pieces. Capture an enemy piece and it flips to your hand — on a later turn you may drop it back onto any empty square instead of moving (crazyhouse rules). Checkmate the enemy King to win.

The start is a 180° rotation: White's back rank a1→d1 is King · Wazir · Dragonknight · Ferz with a pawn on a2; Black is the same shape rotated, so the kings sit on diagonally opposite corners.

How the pieces move

Crazyhouse drops: capturing a piece adds it to your hand (a promoted piece reverts to a pawn). On your turn you may drop a hand piece onto any empty square. Pawns can't be dropped on the 1st or last rank. A drop may give check or mate.
