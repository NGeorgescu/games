Tiny House Chess

4×4 · crazyhouse · fairy pieces

A pocket chess variant on a 4×4 board. Capture a piece and it flips to your hand — drop it back onto any empty square as a move (crazyhouse rules). Back rank, left→right: King · Wazir · Dragonknight · Ferz, with a pawn in front of each king. Play the trained bot or a friend.
